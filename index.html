Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application published by Tobias Heinrich on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Privacy is critically important. The application is designed to be safe and privacy-focused. The application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data, account information, or location statistics.

2. Offline Processing

All application functions and data storage happen strictly offline, locally on your own mobile device. No data is sent to an external server or cloud database.

3. Advertisements and Analytics

The application does not include any advertisement, promotional tracking, or third-party analytical software.

4. Changes to This Policy

If the app features change in the future, this privacy policy page will be updated immediately to clearly explain what data is handled.
